My 2017 Travel Bucket List

  • Maui
  • Reykjavik
  • Aspen

1. Maui

Maui is an island in the Central Pacific, part of the Hawaiian archipelago. Sprawling Haleakala National Park encompasses the highest peak on the island, volcanic Haleakala, as well as the pools and waterfalls, accessed via scenic, winding Hana Highway.

2. Reykjavik

Reykjavik, on the coast of Iceland, is the country's capital and largest city. It's home to the National and Saga museums, tracing the country's Viking history.

3. Aspen

Aspen, located in the Rocky Mountains of Colorado, is a ski resort town and year-round destination for outdoor recreation. It's also known for high-end restaurants and boutiques, and landmarks like the Wheeler Opera House, built in 1889 during the silver mining boom.

Cheers!
